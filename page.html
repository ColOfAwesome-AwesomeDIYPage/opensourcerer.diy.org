Introduction

Authoring a Skill for DIY.org is fun, difficult, and most of all, rewarding. As soon as you see a Maker complete one of the Challenges you’ve made, you’ll feel a warm, fuzzy feeling – like a proud parent. But it’s tricky to get a Skill to be just right. That’s why we’ve compiled this guide for new Skillmakers.

Getting Started

Before you can author your first Skill, you’ll need to send us an email requesting to become a skillmaker. Once setup, you can login at admin.diy.org and get started.
